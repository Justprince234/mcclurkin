Civil litigation

Our trial Attorneys work together to present the best possible case for clients in commercial disputes, breach of contract disputes, intellectual property claims, class action lawsuits, and conflicts with local governments. Our Toronto civil litigation lawyers handle legal disputes involving:

Class Action Lawsuits representing consumers or a "class" of people, or representing the defendant company in complex, nationwide litigation
Shareholder Disputes among business partners, medical practice group members, and investors
Patent Infringement and other intellectual property disputes and violations, including trademark and copyright disputes
Non-compete Agreements, trade secret violations and other employment contracts, representing employers and employees
Water and Sewer Litigation representing municipalities, utility companies, and private companies

Internet Litigation: This is a relatively new area of law and FORSTMANN Solicitors LLP is leading the way in advising clients on strategies to minimize or avoid reputation management problems online, as well as with Internet litigation involving:

Takedown disputes (getting content removed from Web sites, including sites such as YouTube) Domain name disputes,Trademark and copyright disputes over material on the Web Defamation, libel, slander, business disparagement and privacy/publicity claims.

Reputation management is a significant area for many individuals and companies. If you find yourself on the wrong side of technologically adept and unhappy customer or adversary, your business or personal life could face serious problems. It is extremely difficult to get content removed from a Web site but interactive sites do have terms and conditions, rules and laws under which they operate (such as the DMAlabama (Digital Millennium Copyright Act)). There are mechanisms that can be used to provide force to a takedown notice.

Our litigation practice uses state of the art information systems that allow access to all relevant filings, documents, and testimony pertaining to your case with the click of a button. Our investment in technology enhances our ability to present your case to a judge or a jury and to respond to each move the opposition makes.

McClurkin Solicitors provides a strong voice for businesses, employees, and consumers.

When you need effective representation in an Toronto legal dispute, contact a litigation lawyer at McClurkin Solicitors . We have secured successful verdicts and settlements for clients in cases ranging from routine contract disputes to complex litigation. Not only will we bring your initial case to trial; we also handle civil appeals in both state and federal court.

For effective representation in a civil law dispute, contact us today. We represent businesses, local governments, and individuals.

business and corporate law

The founding partners of McClurkin Solicitors , were established business attorneys in Toronto before the firm was formed, and their corporate law practices have been the foundation of the firm's development. Choosing an attorney to handle your business legal issues is as important as choosing a business partner: it takes trust, common goals and communication. Your success is, in part, dependent on the quality of the legal advice you receive, whether it is about a breach of contract or a choice of entity. We serve a cross-section of businesses throughout the country: women- and minority-owned businesses, new and established businesses and business people who need legal advice on problems regarding everything from business formation to breach of contract. Corporate law is a large and diverse legal sector, but with a commercial lawyer that cares for your brand, you can ensure that your business meets the standards of government legislation and conducts its affairs to the letter of the law.

How Can Our Law Firm Help Your Business?

We help our clients in a number of transactional and litigation matters, including:

  • Shareholder or member disputes
  • Noncompete covenants
  • Breach of contract
  • Business formation/choice of entity, such as incorporation (C-corp, S-corp), limited liability company (LLC) or partnership
  • Insurance coverage disputes leveled against insurance companies
  • Lease disputes
  • Directors' and officers' liability
  • Dissolution
  • Reorganization
  • Mergers and acquisitions
  • Joint ventures
  • Trade association membership and activity

Property Law

The firm's real estate attorneys advise and represent clients in connection with real estate transactions and facility issues for all of their office, commercial, and manufacturing needs. With particular expertise in complex commercial and biotech transactions, our team regularly advises on domestic and international facility leasing, acquisition, development, construction, and financing, as well as on all aspects of environmental compliance, due diligence, investigations, and remediation. Our practice also focuses on advising renewable energy clients with respect to project acquisition and development, in addition to representing borrowers and lenders in renewable energy project financings. The deep and broad experience of our team allows the firm to offer its clients timely, efficient, and practical solutions for their real estate and environmental legal issues.

family Law

  • Civil Litigation: breach of contract issues, employment law violations, real estate disputes
  • Personal Injury: Car accidents, collisions with trucks or semis, dog bites, falls or other injuries on poorly maintained properties (premises liability), injuries from the use of defective products (products liability) and medical malpractice claims
  • Complex, High Asset Divorce: division of marital property, retirement plans, estates and business interests, spousal support issues, child custody and support and other family law matters
  • Wealth Management: wills, trusts and other estate planning tools, as well as business succession planning
  • Intellectual Property protection to safeguard privacy, publicity, reputation and copyright interests
  • Real Estate Law, land use and zoning involving land use and zoning
  • Privacy cases and publicity rights
  • Civil Rights
  • Class Actions

energy and utility law

In a world where the demands for energy are growing exponentially, those operating in the energy sector are looking to their lawyers to provide more than legal skill; they are also seeking in-depth sector know-how and innovative solutions to the challenges they face. FORSTMANN Solicitors LLP's energy lawyers deliver to our clients the focused, innovative sector advice they need, wherever in the world they need it. In a sector of relentless change, demand and complexity, private and public corporations wisely rely on experienced, global legal counsel for any matter involving energy.

We are entering an era of unprecedented demand for power generation and transmission, especially within emerging economies. This dynamic, together with the challenges we all face from climate change, is creating new opportunities for alternative energies and new technologies.

Our firm provides legal representation to energy consumers and utility ratepayers in negotiations and court actions.

Our clients include large volume energy consumers such as industrial facilities and educational and medical institutions, as well as municipalities, service providers, and small businesses. We are active in a variety of utility and regulated industry areas, including:

  • Electricity
  • Natural gas
  • Telecommunications
  • Water and sewer
  • Riverboats and charitable gaming institutions
  • Motor carriers and trucking regulations
