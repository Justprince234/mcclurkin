At McClurkin Solicitors , we are always pleased to hear from talented people interested in joining our firm.

Over the course of our 27-year history, the attorneys and professional staff of McClurkin Solicitors have created a unique working environment. With our historical roots in the fast-paced and innovative world, our firm fosters an entrepreneurial spirit and team-oriented approach that you won't find at other law firms. Combine that with our dynamic clients in technology, life sciences, and growth industries and you have a law firm culture unlike any other.

Every day, the employees of McClurkin Solicitors have the opportunity to work with exciting clients striving to develop products and services that will change the world. Our client base includes the who's who of prominent technology companies and other well-known growth enterprises, as well as numerous start-up companies poised to become tomorrow's industry leaders. We offer generous and competitive employee benefits and provide both attorneys and staff with continuous training and career development programs designed to help you achieve your professional goals.

We appreciate your interest in a career at McClurkin Solicitors .
